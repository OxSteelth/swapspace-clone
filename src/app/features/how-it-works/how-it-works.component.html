1. Select the amount and the exchange service

Here is the simple 4-step tutorial on how to exchange cryptocurrency on SwapSpace. Let’s say, you want to swap Ethereum for Bitcoin (in other words, buy some Bitcoin with your Ethereum). Choose ETH in the “You send” and BTC in the “You get” section and enter the amount of ETH you want to exchange. Next, press the ”View offers” button and select the exchange service you like the most.

SwapSpace offers both fixed and floating exchange flows – use the sorting feature or icons next to the rates to choose what suits you best and click on the “Exchange” button.

2. Enter the recipient wallet address

Fill in the “Provide the recipient address” field with the destination Bitcoin address and provide the refund address for your ETH if you want. If your preferred wallet is Trezor, you can press the button to connect a wallet and simplify the exchange process (more on that — in our FAQ). Check the information carefully and click the “Next” button.

3. Transfer your funds

Using the wallet of your choice, send your Ethereum to the address you see on the screen. You can use the QR-code to speed up the process. You can also press the “Connect Wallet” button if you’re using MetaMask, Trezor, or one of the wallets supported by WalletConnect. As soon as our partner gets your ETH, the exchange will continue.

4. Wait until the exchange will be processed

At this stage, you just need to wait until you get your BTC in the wallet. Check the Swap Tracker if you want to know the exchange status, and feel free to contact the support if it seems like something is going wrong. That’s it!
