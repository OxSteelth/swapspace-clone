F.A.Q.

How to create an Invader?

You can mint an Invader by making a swap on SwapSpace.

How to get more cashback?

To increase your cashback, buy a subscription for higher levels.

I have rare 2- or 3-digit Invader. What does it mean?

Rare Invaders will get more generous welcome bonus after the launch of Stage 3. You'll be able to spend your bonus on upgrading the level or customizing the Invader.

How to transfer an Invader to another account?

To do this, you must log in to the wallet from which you minted the Invader. Send this NFT to any address – this address will become your login to your SwapSpace account.

My Invader is inactive – what should I do?

If the first level Invader has not been active for 1 month, its level goes to 0 and it becomes unusable. To activate your level 0 Invader, upgrade it using 100 Diamonds.

What is level down?

If you do not renew your subscription to your current level, your Invader loses one level every month. When the Invader gets to level 0, it becomes inactive until you upgrade it back to level 1.

How to add an eye laser to my Invader?

The eye laser option was only available to those Invaders who were created in the first Stage of our loyalty program. Later Invaders don't have lasers, but you can still decorate them with other great things from our customizer.

How to pay out my cashback?

Go to the Profile tab there you will see your cashback balance. There you can also request a cashback payment. Please note that payouts are available from 10 USDT.
